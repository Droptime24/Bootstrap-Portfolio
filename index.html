About Me

David Luce is a Jr. Developer at Epic SLC; his main duties include web development from start to finish, working mostly on the front end, he does have some experience working with back end operation. David coordinates with the companies graphic designer to ensure proper content is added to the sites. David also oversees hosting account configuration, domain purchasing, cPanel Administration and systems configuration.

Before becoming a Jr. Developer, David helped multiple small businesses scale through, sales, configuration of new systems, structuring corporate trainings, and oversaw company operations. While he worked for Direct Business Lending and Profunding Corporation, David moved from an entry level position to Director of Sales over a four year period, becoming the Operations Manager by year 5.

After a successful career helping people secure funding for their ventures and after having been introduced to so various software and systems, David had found his passion within Information Technology, always pursuing more knowledge,with a firm belief in constant progression. David enjoys remodeling homes and furthering his education in Web Design/Development. He can be reached at (801 991-0135 and is available for consultations.
