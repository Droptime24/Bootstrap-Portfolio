About Me

David Luce is a Jr. Developer at Epic SLC; his main duties include web development from start to finish, working in both the front and back end. He coordinates with the companies graphic designer to ensure proper content is added to the sites. David also oversees hosting account configuration, domain purchasing, cPanel Administration and systems configuration.

Before becoming a Jr. Developer, David helped multiple small businesses scale through, sales, configuration of new systems, structuring corporate trainings, and oversaw company operations.

While he worked for Direct Business Lending and Profunding Corporation, David moved from an entry level position to Director of Sales over a four year period, becoming the Operations Manager by year 5.
